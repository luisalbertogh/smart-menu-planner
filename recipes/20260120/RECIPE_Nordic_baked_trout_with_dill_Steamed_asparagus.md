# Nordic Baked Trout with Dill & Steamed Asparagus

## Ingredients
- 2 trout fillets (150g each)
- 2 tbsp chopped dill
- 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 250g asparagus

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Place trout on baking tray, drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with dill, salt, and pepper. Bake 12 min.
2. Trim asparagus, steam for 5 min until tender.
3. Serve trout with lemon wedges and asparagus.

# Moroccan Chickpea and Vegetable Tagine & Lemon Couscous

## Ingredients
- 200g canned chickpeas
- 1 carrot
- 1 zucchini
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 120g couscous
- 1 lemon
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley

## Instructions
1. Chop vegetables. Saut√© onion, garlic, carrot, zucchini, pepper in olive oil.
2. Add spices, tomatoes, chickpeas. Simmer 20 min.
3. Prepare couscous with boiling water, lemon juice, and parsley.
4. Serve tagine with lemon couscous.

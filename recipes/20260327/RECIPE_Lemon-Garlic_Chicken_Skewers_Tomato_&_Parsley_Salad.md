# Lemon-Garlic Chicken Skewers & Tomato & Parsley Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 chicken breasts (180g each)
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 2 tomatoes
- 1 small red onion
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Cut chicken into cubes, marinate with lemon, garlic, oil, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
2. Thread onto skewers and grill until cooked.
3. Dice tomatoes and onion, toss with parsley, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve skewers with salad.

# Lamb & Vegetable Stew & Labneh with Olive Oil

## Ingredients
- 250g lamb shoulder, diced
- 1 carrot
- 1 celery stalk
- 1 onion
- 1 potato
- 1 tomato
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 400ml vegetable stock
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 100g labneh
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Brown lamb in olive oil, add chopped vegetables and spices.
2. Add stock, simmer 45 min until tender.
3. Serve stew with labneh drizzled with olive oil.

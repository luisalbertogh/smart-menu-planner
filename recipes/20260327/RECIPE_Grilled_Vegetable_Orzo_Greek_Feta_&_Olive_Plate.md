# Grilled Vegetable Orzo & Greek Feta & Olive Plate

## Ingredients
- 120g orzo pasta
- 1 small zucchini
- 1 small eggplant
- 1 red bell pepper
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 80g feta cheese
- 40g Kalamata olives
- 1 lemon
- 1 garlic clove
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook orzo in salted water. Drain and set aside.
2. Dice and grill zucchini, eggplant, and bell pepper with olive oil.
3. Toss grilled vegetables with orzo, lemon juice, and minced garlic.
4. Plate orzo with crumbled feta and olives on the side.
5. Season to taste and serve.

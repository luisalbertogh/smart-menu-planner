# Roasted Eggplant & Feta & Minted Green Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 large eggplant
- 80g feta cheese
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon
- 100g mixed salad greens
- 1 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Slice eggplant, brush with oil, roast at 200°C for 20 min.
2. Crumble feta over roasted eggplant.
3. Toss salad greens with mint, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve eggplant with salad.

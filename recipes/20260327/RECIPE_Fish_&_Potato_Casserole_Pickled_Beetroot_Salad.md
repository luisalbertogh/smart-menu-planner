# Fish & Potato Casserole & Pickled Beetroot Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 white fish fillets (cod/haddock, 150g each)
- 2 medium potatoes
- 1 onion
- 200ml milk
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- 2 cooked beetroots
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Slice potatoes and onion, layer in baking dish with fish.
2. Pour over milk, dot with butter, season, and bake at 190°C for 30 min.
3. Slice beetroots, toss with vinegar, oil, salt, and dill.
4. Serve casserole with beetroot salad.

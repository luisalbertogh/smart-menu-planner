# Chickpea & Spinach Curry & Cucumber Yogurt Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 can chickpeas (400g)
- 150g fresh spinach
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 200ml coconut milk
- 1 cucumber
- 150g Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger in olive oil.
2. Add curry powder, chickpeas, and coconut milk. Simmer 10 min.
3. Add spinach, cook until wilted.
4. For salad: dice cucumber, mix with yogurt, mint, salt, and pepper.
5. Serve curry with salad.

# Stir-Fried Shrimp & Broccoli & Steamed Edamame Beans

## Ingredients
- 200g shrimp, peeled
- 1 small head broccoli
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tsp ginger, grated
- 100g edamame beans (shelled)
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Steam edamame beans and set aside.
2. Stir-fry shrimp with garlic, ginger, and sesame oil.
3. Add broccoli florets, cook until tender.
4. Add soy sauce, toss to coat.
5. Serve with edamame.

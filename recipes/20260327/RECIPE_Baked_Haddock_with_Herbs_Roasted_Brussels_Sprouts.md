# Baked Haddock with Herbs & Roasted Brussels Sprouts

## Ingredients
- 2 haddock fillets (150g each)
- 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 lemon
- 200g Brussels sprouts
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
2. Place haddock on a baking tray, top with herbs, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
3. Halve Brussels sprouts, toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
4. Roast fish and sprouts for 18–20 min until cooked.
5. Serve together.

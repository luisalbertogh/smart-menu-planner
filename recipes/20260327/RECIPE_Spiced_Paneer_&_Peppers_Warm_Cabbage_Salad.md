# Spiced Paneer & Peppers & Warm Cabbage Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g paneer cheese
- 1 red bell pepper
- 1 yellow bell pepper
- 1 onion
- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 200g cabbage
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Cube paneer, slice peppers and onion.
2. Sauté paneer and vegetables with curry powder and oil.
3. Slice cabbage, sauté with oil and vinegar until wilted.
4. Serve paneer and peppers with warm cabbage salad.

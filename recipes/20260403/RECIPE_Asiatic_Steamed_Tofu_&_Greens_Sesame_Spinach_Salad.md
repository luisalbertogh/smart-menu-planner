# Asiatic Steamed Tofu & Greens & Sesame Spinach Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g firm tofu
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 tsp ginger
- 100g baby spinach
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp honey
- 1 green onion
- 100g mixed Asian greens (bok choy, choy sum, etc)

## Instructions
1. Slice tofu, marinate in soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, ginger 10 min. Steam tofu and greens 6 min.
2. Toss spinach with sesame seeds, rice vinegar, honey, sliced green onion.
3. Serve tofu and greens with spinach salad.

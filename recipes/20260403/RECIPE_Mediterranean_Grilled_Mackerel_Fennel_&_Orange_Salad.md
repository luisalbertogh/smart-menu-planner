# Mediterranean Grilled Mackerel & Fennel & Orange Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 mackerel fillets (150g each)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon
- Salt & pepper
- 1 fennel bulb
- 1 orange
- 1 tbsp parsley
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp white wine vinegar

## Instructions
1. Preheat grill. Brush mackerel with oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Grill 4 min per side.
2. Thinly slice fennel, segment orange. Toss with parsley, oil, vinegar, salt.
3. Serve mackerel with salad.

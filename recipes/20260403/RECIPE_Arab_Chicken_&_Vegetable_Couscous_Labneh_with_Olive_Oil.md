# Arab Chicken & Vegetable Couscous & Labneh with Olive Oil

## Ingredients
- 2 chicken thighs (180g each)
- 80g couscous
- 1 carrot
- 1 zucchini
- 1/2 onion
- 1 tomato
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt & pepper
- 100g labneh
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp za'atar (optional)

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion, carrot, zucchini in oil. Add chicken, brown 5 min. Add tomato, spices, salt, pepper, 100ml water. Simmer 20 min.
2. Prepare couscous per package. Serve chicken and veg over couscous.
3. Drizzle labneh with olive oil, sprinkle za'atar.
4. Serve couscous with labneh.

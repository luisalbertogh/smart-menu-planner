# Asiatic Vegetable Fried Rice & Steamed Bok Choy

## Ingredients
- 120g cooked rice
- 1 carrot
- 1/2 red bell pepper
- 1/2 cup peas
- 2 eggs
- 2 green onions
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp ginger
- 1 clove garlic
- 2 heads bok choy
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Dice vegetables. Heat sesame oil, sauté ginger, garlic, carrot, pepper, peas 3 min.
2. Push veggies aside, scramble eggs. Add rice, soy sauce, green onions, stir-fry 3 min.
3. Steam bok choy 4 min, sprinkle with sesame seeds.
4. Serve fried rice with bok choy.

# Nordic Baked Trout & Roasted Fennel & Carrots

## Ingredients
- 2 trout fillets (150g each)
- 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Fresh dill
- Salt & pepper
- 1 fennel bulb
- 2 carrots
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp thyme

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Place trout on baking tray, drizzle with olive oil, lemon juice, dill, salt, and pepper. Bake 15 min.
2. Slice fennel and carrots, toss with olive oil, thyme, salt, and pepper. Roast 25 min at 190°C.
3. Serve trout with roasted vegetables.

# Hindic Red Lentil & Spinach Dal & Carrot & Mint Raita

## Ingredients
- 100g red lentils
- 1 small onion
- 1 tomato
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 tsp ginger
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tbsp oil
- 60g spinach
- Salt
- 1 carrot
- 100g plain yogurt
- 1 tbsp chopped mint
- 1/2 tsp cumin powder
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Rinse lentils. Sauté onion, garlic, ginger, cumin seeds in oil. Add tomato, turmeric, lentils, 300ml water. Simmer 15 min.
2. Add spinach, garam masala, salt. Cook 5 min.
3. Grate carrot, mix with yogurt, mint, cumin powder, salt for raita.
4. Serve dal with raita.

# Nordic Salmon & Barley Pilaf & Pickled Cabbage Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 salmon fillets (150g each)
- 80g pearl barley
- 1 small onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 celery stalk
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp dill
- Salt & pepper
- 100g red cabbage
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp parsley

## Instructions
1. Cook barley in salted water 25 min. Sauté onion, carrot, celery in oil, add barley, dill, salt, pepper.
2. Bake salmon at 180°C for 15 min.
3. Shred cabbage, toss with vinegar, sugar, parsley, salt. Let sit 10 min.
4. Serve salmon with pilaf and salad.

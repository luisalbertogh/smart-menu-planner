# Arab Spiced Grilled Chicken & Tomato & Cucumber Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 chicken breasts (180g each)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- Salt & pepper
- 2 tomatoes
- 1 cucumber
- 1/2 red onion
- 1 tbsp parsley
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Mix oil, spices, salt, and pepper. Coat chicken, grill 6 min per side.
2. Dice tomatoes, cucumber, onion. Toss with parsley, lemon juice, salt.
3. Serve chicken with salad.

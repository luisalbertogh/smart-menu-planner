# Mediterranean Stuffed Peppers & Greek Yogurt & Herb Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 large bell peppers
- 80g cooked brown rice
- 80g cooked lentils
- 1 small onion
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 tomato
- 40g feta cheese
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt & pepper
- 150g Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
- 1 tbsp chopped fresh mint
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1/2 cucumber
- Lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Halve and core peppers.
2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil. Add tomato, rice, lentils, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook 5 min.
3. Stuff peppers with mixture, top with feta. Bake 25 min.
4. For salad: Mix yogurt, herbs, diced cucumber, lemon juice, salt. Serve chilled.

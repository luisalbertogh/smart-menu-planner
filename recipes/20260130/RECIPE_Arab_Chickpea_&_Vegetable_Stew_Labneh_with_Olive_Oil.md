# Arab Chickpea & Vegetable Stew & Labneh with Olive Oil

## Ingredients
- 1 can chickpeas (400g)
- 1 small eggplant, diced
- 1 small zucchini, diced
- 1 small carrot, diced
- 1/2 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 200g labneh
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for labneh)
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a pot, saut√© onion and garlic.
2. Add eggplant, zucchini, carrot, and cook until soft.
3. Add chickpeas, cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, and pepper. Simmer 10 min.
4. Serve stew with labneh drizzled with olive oil.

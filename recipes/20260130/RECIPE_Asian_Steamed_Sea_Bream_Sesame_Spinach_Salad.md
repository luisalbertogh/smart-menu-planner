# Asian Steamed Sea Bream & Sesame Spinach Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 sea bream fillet (160g man, 120g woman)
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1/2 tsp rice vinegar
- 200g fresh spinach
- 1 tsp sesame seeds
- 1 spring onion, sliced

## Instructions
1. Place sea bream on a plate, top with soy sauce, ginger, and garlic. Steam for 8-10 min.
2. Wilt spinach in a pan with sesame oil, add rice vinegar and sesame seeds.
3. Serve fish with spinach salad, garnish with spring onion.

# Mediterranean Pasta with Sardines & Fennel & Orange Salad

## Ingredients
- 120g whole wheat pasta
- 1 can sardines in olive oil (120g)
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/2 lemon, zest & juice
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 small fennel bulb, thinly sliced
- 1 orange, segmented
- 1 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
- 1 tsp olive oil (for salad)

## Instructions
1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
2. In a pan, heat olive oil, saut√© garlic, add sardines, lemon zest, and juice. Toss with pasta and parsley.
3. Mix fennel, orange, mint, and olive oil for salad.
4. Serve pasta with salad.

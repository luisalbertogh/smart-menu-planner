# Mediterranean Vegetable Rice & Greek Yogurt & Herb Dip

## Ingredients
- 120g basmati rice
- 1 small zucchini, diced
- 1 small red bell pepper, diced
- 1 small carrot, diced
- 1/2 small red onion, diced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 tsp dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 200g Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 tsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Cook the rice according to package instructions.
2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© onion and garlic until fragrant.
3. Add zucchini, bell pepper, and carrot. Cook until tender.
4. Stir in oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix with cooked rice.
5. For the dip, combine Greek yogurt, dill, parsley, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
6. Serve the rice with the yogurt dip on the side.

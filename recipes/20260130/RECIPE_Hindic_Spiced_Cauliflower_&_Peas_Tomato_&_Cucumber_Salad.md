# Hindic Spiced Cauliflower & Peas & Tomato & Cucumber Salad

## Ingredients
- 1/2 head cauliflower, cut into florets
- 1 cup frozen peas
- 1 small potato, diced
- 1/2 onion, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1/2 cucumber, diced
- 1 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
- 1 tsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, then onion and garlic. Saut√©.
2. Add cauliflower, potato, peas, garam masala, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Cook until tender.
3. Mix tomatoes, cucumber, coriander, lemon juice, salt, and pepper for salad.
4. Serve spiced cauliflower and peas with salad.

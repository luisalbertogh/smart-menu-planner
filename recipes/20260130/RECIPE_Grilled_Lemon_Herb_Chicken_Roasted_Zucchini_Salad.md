# Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken & Roasted Zucchini Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 chicken breasts (180g man, 140g woman)
- 1 lemon (zest & juice)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 small zucchinis, sliced
- 1/2 red onion, sliced
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

## Instructions
1. Marinate chicken with lemon zest, juice, olive oil, oregano, thyme, garlic, salt, and pepper for 30 min.
2. Grill chicken until cooked through.
3. Toss zucchini and onion with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 200Â°C for 20 min.
4. Mix roasted veggies with balsamic vinegar and parsley.
5. Serve chicken with zucchini salad.

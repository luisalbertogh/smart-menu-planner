# Nordic Salmon & Root Veggie Bake & Cabbage & Apple Slaw

## Ingredients
- 1 salmon fillet (180g man, 140g woman)
- 1 small potato, diced
- 1 small carrot, diced
- 1/2 parsnip, diced
- 1/2 small red onion, sliced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp dried dill
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 cup shredded cabbage
- 1 small apple, julienned
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp honey
- 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Toss potato, carrot, parsnip, and onion with olive oil, dill, salt, and pepper. Bake 20 min.
2. Place salmon on top, season, and bake 12 min more.
3. Mix cabbage, apple, vinegar, honey, and dill for slaw.
4. Serve salmon bake with slaw.

# Grilled Halloumi & Eggplant & Minted Green Salad

## Ingredients
- 180g halloumi cheese
- 1 small eggplant, sliced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 cups mixed salad greens
- 1 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for salad)

## Instructions
1. Brush eggplant with olive oil, season with oregano, salt, and pepper. Grill until tender.
2. Grill halloumi until golden.
3. Toss salad greens with mint, lemon juice, and olive oil.
4. Serve halloumi and eggplant with salad.

# Nordic Chicken & Barley Soup & Pickled Beetroot Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 chicken breast (180g man, 140g woman)
- 1/2 cup pearl barley
- 1 small carrot, diced
- 1 small parsnip, diced
- 1/2 leek, sliced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1.2L chicken stock
- 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 small cooked beetroots, sliced
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp honey
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

## Instructions
1. In a pot, combine chicken, barley, carrot, parsnip, leek, garlic, and stock. Simmer 30 min.
2. Remove chicken, shred, return to soup. Add dill, salt, and pepper.
3. Mix beetroot, vinegar, honey, and parsley for salad.
4. Serve soup with salad.

# Arab Lamb & Vegetable Couscous with Labneh with Olive Oil

## Ingredients
- 150g lamb leg, diced
- 1/2 small zucchini
- 1/2 small carrot
- 1/2 small red bell pepper
- 1/2 small onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 80g couscous
- 150ml vegetable broth
- 100g labneh
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for labneh)
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil. Add lamb and spices, brown on all sides.
2. Add diced vegetables, cook 5 minutes.
3. Add broth, simmer 10 minutes. Prepare couscous per package instructions.
4. Serve lamb and vegetables over couscous. Drizzle labneh with olive oil as side.

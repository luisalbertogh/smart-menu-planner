# Nordic Fish & Root Vegetable Stew with Rye Bread & Butter

## Ingredients
- 2 white fish fillets (cod/haddock, 250g total)
- 1 small potato
- 1 small carrot
- 1 small parsnip
- 1/2 leek
- 400ml fish or vegetable stock
- 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- 2 slices rye bread
- 20g butter

## Instructions
1. Dice all vegetables. Sauté leek in olive oil until soft.
2. Add diced root vegetables, cook 3 minutes.
3. Add stock, simmer 10 minutes.
4. Add fish, simmer 5 minutes until cooked. Stir in dill.
5. Serve stew hot with rye bread and butter on the side.

# Hindic Chicken & Spinach Curry with Cucumber Mint Raita

## Ingredients
- 1 large chicken breast (200g)
- 150g fresh spinach
- 1 small onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp ginger, grated
- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 200ml coconut milk
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- 1/2 cucumber
- 100g Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
- 1/2 lemon (juice)

## Instructions
1. Dice the chicken breast and season with salt and pepper.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté chopped onion, garlic, and ginger until soft.
3. Add chicken and curry powder, cook until browned.
4. Add spinach and coconut milk, simmer 10 minutes.
5. For raita: grate cucumber, squeeze excess water, mix with yogurt, mint, and lemon juice.
6. Serve curry hot with raita on the side.

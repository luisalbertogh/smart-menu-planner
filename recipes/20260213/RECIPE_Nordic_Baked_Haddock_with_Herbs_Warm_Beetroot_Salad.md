# Nordic Baked Haddock with Herbs & Warm Beetroot Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 haddock fillets (250g total)
- 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 lemon (zest & juice)
- 2 medium beetroots
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp honey
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place haddock fillets on a baking tray, top with herbs, lemon zest, salt, and pepper.
2. Bake for 15 minutes.
3. Boil beetroots until tender, peel and slice.
4. Toss beetroot with olive oil, mustard, honey, and lemon juice. Serve warm with baked haddock.

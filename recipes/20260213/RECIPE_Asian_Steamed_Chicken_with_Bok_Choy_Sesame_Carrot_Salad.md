# Asian Steamed Chicken with Bok Choy & Sesame Carrot Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 large chicken breast (200g)
- 2 heads bok choy
- 1 small carrot
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame seeds
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Slice chicken breast, marinate with soy sauce, ginger, and garlic for 10 minutes.
2. Steam chicken and bok choy until cooked (about 8 minutes).
3. Grate carrot, toss with sesame oil, vinegar, sesame seeds, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve chicken and bok choy with carrot salad on the side.

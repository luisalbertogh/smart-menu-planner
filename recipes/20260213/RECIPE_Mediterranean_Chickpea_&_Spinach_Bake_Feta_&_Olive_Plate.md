# Mediterranean Chickpea & Spinach Bake with Feta & Olive Plate

## Ingredients
- 200g cooked chickpeas
- 150g fresh spinach
- 1 small onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 egg
- 50g feta cheese
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 40g black olives
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft. Add spinach, cook until wilted.
2. Mix with chickpeas, egg, oregano, salt, and pepper. Pour into baking dish, top with crumbled feta.
3. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes.
4. Serve with olives on the side.

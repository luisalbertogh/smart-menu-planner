# Mediterranean Vegetable & Rice Pilaf with Greek Yogurt with Herbs

## Ingredients
- 100g basmati rice
- 1 small zucchini
- 1 small red bell pepper
- 1 small carrot
- 1 small onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 200ml vegetable broth
- 100g Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Dice all vegetables. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
2. Add remaining vegetables, cook 5 minutes.
3. Stir in rice, oregano, and broth. Simmer covered 15 minutes.
4. Mix yogurt with parsley, salt, and pepper. Serve pilaf with herbed yogurt.

# Arab Spiced Grilled Eggplant with Tomato & Parsley Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 large eggplant
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- Salt & pepper
- 2 medium tomatoes
- 1/2 small red onion
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Slice eggplant, brush with olive oil, and sprinkle with spices, salt, and pepper.
2. Grill eggplant slices until tender and charred.
3. Dice tomatoes and onion, mix with parsley and lemon juice. Serve salad with grilled eggplant.

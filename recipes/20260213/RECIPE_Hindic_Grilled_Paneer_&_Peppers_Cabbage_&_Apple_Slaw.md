# Hindic Grilled Paneer & Peppers with Cabbage & Apple Slaw

## Ingredients
- 150g paneer cheese
- 1 small red bell pepper
- 1 small yellow bell pepper
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp garam masala
- Salt & pepper
- 1/4 small cabbage
- 1 small apple
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp Greek yogurt

## Instructions
1. Cut paneer and peppers into chunks, toss with olive oil, garam masala, salt, and pepper.
2. Grill until golden and peppers are tender.
3. Shred cabbage and apple, toss with lemon juice and yogurt.
4. Serve grilled paneer and peppers with slaw.

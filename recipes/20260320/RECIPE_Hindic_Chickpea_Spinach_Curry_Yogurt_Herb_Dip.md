# Hindic Chickpea & Spinach Curry & Yogurt & Herb Dip

## Ingredients
- 150g chickpeas (cooked)
- 100g spinach
- 1 onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 120g yogurt
- 1 tbsp fresh herbs (mint, coriander)

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
2. Add chickpeas, curry powder, cook 5 min.
3. Add spinach, cook until wilted.
4. Mix yogurt with chopped herbs.
5. Serve curry with herb dip.

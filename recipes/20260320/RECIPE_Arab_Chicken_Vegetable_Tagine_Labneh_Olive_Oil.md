# Arab Chicken & Vegetable Tagine & Labneh with Olive Oil

## Ingredients
- 200g chicken breast
- 1 carrot
- 1 zucchini
- 1 onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 120g labneh
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for labneh)

## Instructions
1. Cut chicken and vegetables into chunks.
2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil, add chicken, brown.
3. Add vegetables and spices, cook 15 min.
4. Serve with labneh drizzled with olive oil.

# Asiatic Steamed Pollock with Greens & Sesame Spinach Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g pollock fillet
- 100g spinach
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds
- 1/2 bok choy (100g)

## Instructions
1. Steam pollock and bok choy for 8 min.
2. Toss spinach with sesame oil and seeds.
3. Drizzle soy sauce over fish and greens.
4. Serve pollock with spinach salad.

# Nordic Roasted Cauliflower & Cod & Watercress & Radish Salad

## Ingredients
- 250g cod fillet
- 1/2 cauliflower (300g)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- 50g watercress
- 4 radishes
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
2. Cut cauliflower into florets, toss with olive oil, salt, pepper, roast 20 min.
3. Season cod, roast 12 min.
4. Slice radishes, mix with watercress and lemon juice.
5. Serve cod and cauliflower with salad.

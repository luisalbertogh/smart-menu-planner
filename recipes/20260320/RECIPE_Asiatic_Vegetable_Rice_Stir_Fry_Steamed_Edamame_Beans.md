# Asiatic Vegetable & Rice Stir-Fry & Steamed Edamame Beans

## Ingredients
- 120g jasmine rice
- 1 carrot
- 1 bell pepper
- 1/2 broccoli (150g)
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 100g edamame beans

## Instructions
1. Cook rice as per package.
2. Chop vegetables, stir-fry in sesame oil, add soy sauce.
3. Steam edamame beans 5 min.
4. Serve stir-fry with rice and edamame.

# Arab Spiced Lamb Kebabs & Minted Cucumber Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g lamb mince
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt & pepper
- 1 cucumber
- 1 tbsp fresh mint
- 1 tbsp yogurt

## Instructions
1. Mix lamb with spices, form kebabs.
2. Grill kebabs 8 min, turning.
3. Slice cucumber, mix with mint and yogurt.
4. Serve kebabs with salad.

# Nordic Salmon & Potato Bake & Pickled Beetroot Salad

## Ingredients
- 250g salmon fillet
- 2 medium potatoes
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- 100g beetroot (cooked)
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 1 tbsp dill

## Instructions
1. Slice potatoes, layer in baking dish, drizzle olive oil, bake 20 min at 200°C.
2. Add salmon, bake 12 min.
3. Slice beetroot, mix with vinegar and dill.
4. Serve salmon bake with beetroot salad.

# Mediterranean Grilled Eggplant Rolls & Tomato & Basil Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 large eggplant
- 100g ricotta cheese
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 medium tomatoes
- 1 tbsp fresh basil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Slice eggplant, brush with olive oil, grill 3 min per side.
2. Spread ricotta on eggplant slices, roll up.
3. Slice tomatoes, mix with basil, olive oil, salt, pepper.
4. Serve eggplant rolls with salad.

# Mediterranean Grilled Sea Bass & Greek Feta & Tomato Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 whole sea bass (400g, cleaned)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon
- Salt & pepper
- 120g Greek feta cheese
- 2 medium tomatoes
- 1/2 red onion
- 1 tbsp fresh oregano
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for salad)

## Instructions
1. Preheat grill to medium-high.
2. Rub sea bass with olive oil, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
3. Grill sea bass 5-7 min per side until cooked.
4. Slice tomatoes and onion, mix with feta, oregano, and olive oil.
5. Serve sea bass with salad.

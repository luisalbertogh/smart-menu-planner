# Italian Pasta e Fagioli with Arugula & Parmesan Salad

## Ingredients
- 80g small pasta (ditalini or elbow)
- 200g canned cannellini beans
- 1 small carrot
- 1 celery stalk
- 1/2 onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 400ml vegetable broth
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 bay leaf
- 40g arugula
- 20g parmesan cheese
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Saut√© onion, garlic, carrot, and celery in olive oil until soft.
2. Add beans, bay leaf, and broth. Simmer 10 minutes.
3. Add pasta, cook until al dente. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Toss arugula with parmesan and balsamic vinegar. Serve soup with salad.

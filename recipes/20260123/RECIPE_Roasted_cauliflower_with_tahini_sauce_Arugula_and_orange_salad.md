# Roasted cauliflower with tahini sauce & Arugula and orange salad

## Ingredients
- 1 small cauliflower
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 1 tbsp tahini
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp water
- 1/2 garlic clove
- 40g arugula
- 1 orange
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for salad)

## Instructions
1. Cut cauliflower into florets, toss with oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 200Â°C for 25 min.
2. Mix tahini, lemon juice, water, and garlic for sauce.
3. Slice orange, toss with arugula and olive oil.
4. Serve cauliflower with tahini sauce and salad.

# Steamed hake with parsley sauce & Sautéed green beans

## Ingredients
- 2 hake fillets (120g each)
- 1/2 lemon
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 1/2 bunch parsley
- 1/2 garlic clove
- 50ml light cream
- 200g green beans
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for beans)

## Instructions
1. Steam hake fillets with lemon slices until cooked.
2. Blend parsley, garlic, cream, olive oil, salt, and pepper for sauce.
3. Sauté green beans in olive oil until tender.
4. Serve hake with parsley sauce and green beans.

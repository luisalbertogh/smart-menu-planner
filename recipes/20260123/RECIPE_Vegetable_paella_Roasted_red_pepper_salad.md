# Vegetable paella & Roasted red pepper salad

## Ingredients
- 150g paella rice
- 1/2 red bell pepper
- 1/2 green bell pepper
- 1/2 zucchini
- 1/2 eggplant
- 1 tomato
- 1/2 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 400ml vegetable broth
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/4 tsp saffron
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 2 red bell peppers (for salad)
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for salad)
- 1 tsp sherry vinegar
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley

## Instructions
1. Saut√© onion, garlic, and chopped vegetables in olive oil.
2. Add rice, paprika, saffron, and stir. Add broth and simmer until rice is cooked.
3. Season and let rest 5 minutes.
4. Roast red peppers, peel, slice, and toss with oil, vinegar, parsley, salt, and pepper.

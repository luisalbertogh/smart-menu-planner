# Nordic Fish Cakes (Haddock) with Pickled Beetroot Salad

## Ingredients
- 250g haddock fillet
- 1 small potato
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1 tbsp chopped dill
- 1/2 lemon (zest)
- 1 tbsp oil
- 2 small cooked beetroots
- 1/2 red onion
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 1 tsp sugar
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Boil potato, mash. Flake haddock, mix with potato, egg, flour, dill, lemon zest, salt, and pepper.
2. Form into cakes, fry in oil until golden.
3. Slice beetroots and onion, toss with vinegar, sugar, and a pinch of salt.
4. Serve fish cakes with pickled beetroot salad.

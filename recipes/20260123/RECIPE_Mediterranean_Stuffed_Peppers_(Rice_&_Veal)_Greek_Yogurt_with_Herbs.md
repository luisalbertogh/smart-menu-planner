# Mediterranean Stuffed Peppers (Rice & Veal) with Greek Yogurt with Herbs

## Ingredients
- 2 large bell peppers
- 120g ground veal
- 80g rice
- 1 small onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tomato
- 1 tbsp parsley
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 150g Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp chopped mint
- 1 tbsp chopped dill
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Cut tops off peppers, remove seeds.
2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil, add veal, cook until browned.
3. Add rice, diced tomato, parsley, salt, and pepper. Cook 2 minutes.
4. Stuff peppers with mixture, place in baking dish, add a splash of water. Cover and bake 35 minutes.
5. Mix Greek yogurt with mint and dill. Serve stuffed peppers with herbed yogurt.

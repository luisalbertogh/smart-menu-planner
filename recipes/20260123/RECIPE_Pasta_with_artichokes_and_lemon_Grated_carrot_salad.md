# Pasta with artichokes and lemon & Grated carrot salad

## Ingredients
- 150g short pasta (penne, fusilli)
- 1 can artichoke hearts (drained)
- 1 lemon (zest and juice)
- 1 garlic clove
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- Salt and pepper
- 3 medium carrots
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for salad)
- 1 tsp lemon juice (for salad)
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook pasta, drain. Saut√© garlic in olive oil, add artichokes, lemon zest, and juice.
2. Toss pasta with artichoke mixture and parsley.
3. Grate carrots, toss with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve pasta with carrot salad.

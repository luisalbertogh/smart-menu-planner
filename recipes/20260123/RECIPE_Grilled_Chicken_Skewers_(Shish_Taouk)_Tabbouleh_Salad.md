# Grilled Chicken Skewers (Shish Taouk) with Tabbouleh Salad

## Ingredients
- 250g chicken breast
- 1 tbsp yogurt
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp coriander
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 bunch parsley
- 1 medium tomato
- 1/2 cucumber
- 2 spring onions
- 40g bulgur wheat
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Cut chicken into cubes, marinate with yogurt, lemon, garlic, paprika, cumin, coriander, olive oil, salt, and pepper for 30 min.
2. Thread onto skewers, grill until cooked through.
3. Chop parsley, tomato, cucumber, and spring onions. Mix with soaked bulgur, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve chicken skewers with tabbouleh.

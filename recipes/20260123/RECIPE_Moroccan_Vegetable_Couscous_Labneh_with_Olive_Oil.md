# Moroccan Vegetable Couscous with Labneh & Olive Oil

## Ingredients
- 120g couscous
- 1 small zucchini
- 1 small carrot
- 1/2 red bell pepper
- 1/2 onion
- 1 tbsp raisins
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp coriander
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 200g labneh
- 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Chop all vegetables. Saut√© onion, carrot, zucchini, and bell pepper in olive oil until soft.
2. Add spices and raisins, cook 1 minute.
3. Add 150ml water, bring to boil. Stir in couscous, cover, remove from heat, let steam 5 minutes.
4. Fluff couscous, season with salt and pepper.
5. Serve with labneh drizzled with olive oil.

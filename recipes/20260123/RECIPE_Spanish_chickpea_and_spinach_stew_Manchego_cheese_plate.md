# Spanish chickpea and spinach stew & Manchego cheese plate

## Ingredients
- 200g dried chickpeas (or 400g canned)
- 200g fresh spinach
- 1 medium onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 medium carrot
- 1 celery stalk
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 400ml vegetable broth
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 100g Manchego cheese (sliced)
- 6-8 whole grain crackers

## Instructions
1. If using dried chickpeas, soak overnight and cook until tender. If using canned, drain and rinse.
2. Heat olive oil in a pot, saut√© onion, garlic, carrot, and celery until soft.
3. Add smoked paprika, stir, then add chickpeas and broth. Simmer 15 minutes.
4. Add spinach, cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Serve stew hot. Plate Manchego cheese with crackers on the side.

# Indian dal tadka with basmati rice & Cucumber and yogurt salad

## Ingredients
- 120g red lentils
- 1/2 onion
- 1 tomato
- 1 garlic clove
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
- 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 300ml water
- Salt
- 100g basmati rice
- 1/2 cucumber
- 100g plain yogurt
- 1 tbsp chopped coriander
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook lentils with onion, tomato, garlic, turmeric, and water until soft.
2. Heat oil, fry cumin and mustard seeds, add to lentils with garam masala and salt.
3. Cook rice separately.
4. Mix cucumber, yogurt, coriander, salt, and pepper for salad.
5. Serve dal with rice and salad.

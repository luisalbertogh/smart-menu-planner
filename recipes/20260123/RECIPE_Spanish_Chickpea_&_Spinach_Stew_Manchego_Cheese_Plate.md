# Spanish Chickpea & Spinach Stew with Manchego Cheese Plate

## Ingredients
- 200g dried chickpeas (or 400g canned)
- 200g fresh spinach
- 1 medium onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 medium carrot
- 1 celery stalk
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 500ml vegetable broth
- Salt & pepper
- 80g Manchego cheese (sliced)

## Instructions
1. If using dried chickpeas, soak overnight and cook until tender. If using canned, drain and rinse.
2. Heat olive oil in a pot, saut√© onion, garlic, carrot, and celery until soft.
3. Add smoked paprika, bay leaf, and chickpeas. Stir for 2 minutes.
4. Add broth, bring to a simmer for 15 minutes.
5. Add spinach, cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Serve stew hot with Manchego cheese slices on the side.

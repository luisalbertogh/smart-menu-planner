# Asian Steamed Cod with Ginger & Stir-fried Bok Choy

## Ingredients
- 2 cod fillets (150g each)
- 1 thumb ginger
- 2 spring onions
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 head bok choy
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Place cod fillets on a plate, top with sliced ginger and spring onions. Steam for 8-10 minutes.
2. Drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil.
3. Stir-fry bok choy with garlic in vegetable oil for 2-3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Serve cod with bok choy.

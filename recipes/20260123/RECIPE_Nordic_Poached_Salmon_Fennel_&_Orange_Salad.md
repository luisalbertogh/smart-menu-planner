# Nordic Poached Salmon with Fennel & Orange Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 salmon fillets (180g each)
- 1 lemon (sliced)
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tsp black peppercorns
- 1 small fennel bulb
- 1 orange
- 1 tbsp fresh dill
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt

## Instructions
1. In a pan, add water, lemon slices, bay leaf, and peppercorns. Bring to a simmer.
2. Add salmon fillets, poach gently for 8-10 minutes.
3. Thinly slice fennel and orange, toss with dill, olive oil, and a pinch of salt.
4. Serve salmon with fennel & orange salad.

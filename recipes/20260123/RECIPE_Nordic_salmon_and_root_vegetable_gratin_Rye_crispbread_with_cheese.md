# Nordic salmon and root vegetable gratin & Rye crispbread with cheese

## Ingredients
- 250g salmon fillet
- 2 medium potatoes
- 1 parsnip
- 1 carrot
- 1 small leek
- 200ml light cream
- 1 tbsp dill
- 2 tbsp grated cheese
- 2 rye crispbreads
- 40g semi-hard cheese (e.g., Havarti)
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 190Â°C. Slice potatoes, parsnip, carrot, and leek thinly.
2. Layer vegetables and salmon in a baking dish. Sprinkle with dill, salt, and pepper.
3. Pour cream over, top with grated cheese. Bake 25-30 minutes.
4. Serve with rye crispbread and cheese slices.

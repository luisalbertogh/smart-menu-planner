# Grilled chicken skewers (Shish Taouk) & Fattoush salad

## Ingredients
- 2 chicken breasts (300g total)
- 2 tbsp yogurt
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 1 small romaine lettuce
- 1 tomato
- 1/2 cucumber
- 1/2 red onion
- 1/2 bell pepper
- 1/2 pita bread
- 1 tbsp sumac
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for salad)
- 1 tbsp lemon juice (for salad)

## Instructions
1. Cut chicken into cubes. Marinate with yogurt, lemon, garlic, paprika, cumin, oil, salt, and pepper for 1 hour.
2. Thread onto skewers and grill until cooked through.
3. Chop salad vegetables and pita. Toss with sumac, parsley, olive oil, and lemon juice.
4. Serve chicken skewers with fattoush salad.

# Lemon baked cod with fennel & Warm beetroot salad

## Ingredients
- 2 cod fillets (150g each)
- 1 lemon (zest and juice)
- 1 fennel bulb
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 2 medium beetroots
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Slice fennel and place in baking dish.
2. Lay cod fillets on top, drizzle with olive oil, lemon juice, and zest. Season.
3. Bake for 15-18 minutes until fish is cooked.
4. Boil beetroots until tender, peel and slice.
5. Toss beets with olive oil, vinegar, parsley, salt, and pepper. Serve warm.

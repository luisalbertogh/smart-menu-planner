# Indian Palak Paneer with Tomato & Onion Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g paneer
- 250g spinach
- 1 medium tomato
- 1/2 onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 thumb ginger
- 1 green chili (optional)
- 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Blanch spinach, blend to a puree.
2. Saut√© cumin seeds in oil, add chopped onion, garlic, ginger, and chili. Cook until soft.
3. Add tomato, turmeric, garam masala, cook 2 min.
4. Add spinach puree, cubed paneer, simmer 5 min. Season with salt.
5. Slice tomato and onion, toss with lemon juice and salt. Serve with palak paneer.

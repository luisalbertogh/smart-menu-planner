# Nordic Salmon & Potato Bake with Pickled Beetroot Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 salmon fillets (120g each)
- 3 medium potatoes
- 1 small onion
- 1 tbsp fresh dill
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cooked beetroots
- 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Slice potatoes and onion, layer in a baking dish, drizzle with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Bake 20 min.
2. Place salmon on top, sprinkle with dill, bake 12 min more.
3. Slice beetroots, toss with vinegar, sugar, parsley, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve salmon bake with pickled beetroot salad.

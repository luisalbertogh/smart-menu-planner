# Asian Ginger Steamed Trout with Sesame Broccoli Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 trout fillets (120g each)
- 1 thumb ginger
- 2 spring onions
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 head broccoli
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Place trout fillets on a plate, top with sliced ginger and spring onions. Steam for 8-10 min.
2. Cut broccoli into florets, steam until just tender. Toss with sesame oil, olive oil, sesame seeds, salt, and pepper.
3. Mix soy sauce and rice vinegar, drizzle over trout before serving. Serve with broccoli salad.

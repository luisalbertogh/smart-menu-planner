# Nordic Baked Cod with Dill & Roasted Carrot & Fennel

## Ingredients
- 2 cod fillets (150g each)
- 1 bunch fresh dill
- 1 lemon
- 2 carrots
- 1 fennel bulb
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Slice carrots and fennel, toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast 25 min.
2. Place cod fillets in a baking dish, top with chopped dill, lemon slices, salt, and pepper. Drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil.
3. Bake cod for 12-15 min until just cooked.
4. Serve cod with roasted vegetables on the side.

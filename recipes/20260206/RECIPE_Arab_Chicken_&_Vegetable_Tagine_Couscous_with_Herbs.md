# Arab Chicken & Vegetable Tagine with Couscous with Herbs

## Ingredients
- 2 chicken thighs (bone-in, skinless)
- 1 zucchini
- 1 carrot
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 200g canned tomatoes
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 100g couscous
- 1 bunch fresh parsley
- 1 bunch fresh mint
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a pot, brown chicken thighs. Remove and set aside.
2. Saut√© chopped onion, garlic, carrot, zucchini, and bell pepper until soft.
3. Add spices, cook 1 min. Add tomatoes and chicken, cover and simmer 30 min.
4. Prepare couscous as per package, fluff with fork, mix in chopped parsley and mint, 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper.
5. Serve tagine hot with herbed couscous.

# Mediterranean Chickpea & Spinach Stew with Grilled Halloumi Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g dried chickpeas (or 400g canned)
- 200g fresh spinach
- 1 medium onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 carrot
- 1 celery stalk
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- 150g halloumi cheese
- 1 small mixed salad (lettuce, tomato, cucumber)
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. If using dried chickpeas, soak overnight and cook until tender. If using canned, drain and rinse.
2. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a pot, saut√© chopped onion, garlic, carrot, and celery until soft.
3. Add cumin and paprika, cook 1 min. Add tomatoes and chickpeas, simmer 15 min.
4. Add spinach, cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Slice halloumi, grill in a pan until golden.
6. Toss salad with lemon juice and 1 tbsp olive oil. Top with grilled halloumi.
7. Serve stew hot with salad on the side.

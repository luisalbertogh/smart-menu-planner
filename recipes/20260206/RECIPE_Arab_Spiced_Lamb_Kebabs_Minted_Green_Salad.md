# Arab Spiced Lamb Kebabs with Minted Green Salad

## Ingredients
- 250g ground lamb
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- 1 small lettuce
- 1/2 cucumber
- 1 tbsp fresh mint
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Mix lamb with spices, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper. Shape into kebabs.
2. Grill or pan-fry kebabs in olive oil until cooked through.
3. Chop lettuce and cucumber, toss with mint, lemon juice, and a pinch of salt.
4. Serve kebabs with minted salad.

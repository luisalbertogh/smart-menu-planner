# Mediterranean Pasta with Sardines & Arugula & Parmesan Salad

## Ingredients
- 160g pasta (penne or spaghetti)
- 1 can sardines in olive oil (120g)
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 50g arugula
- 20g parmesan cheese
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook pasta in salted water, drain.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sautÃ© garlic, add sardines, break up gently.
3. Add cooked pasta, lemon zest, juice, and parsley, toss to combine.
4. Toss arugula with balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Shave parmesan on top.
5. Serve pasta hot with salad on the side.

# Mediterranean Grilled Eggplant Rolls with Tomato & Olive Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 large eggplant
- 100g ricotta cheese
- 1 tbsp chopped basil
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tomatoes
- 50g black olives
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Slice eggplant lengthwise, brush with olive oil, grill until soft.
2. Mix ricotta with basil and parsley, season with salt and pepper.
3. Spread ricotta mix on eggplant slices, roll up.
4. Slice tomatoes, mix with olives, olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
5. Serve eggplant rolls with tomato & olive salad.

# Hindic Lentil & Vegetable Curry with Cucumber Raita

## Ingredients
- 150g dried lentils
- 1 potato
- 1 carrot
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 thumb ginger
- 1 tomato
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 200g plain yogurt
- 1/2 cucumber
- 1 tbsp fresh coriander
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Rinse lentils, cook in water until tender. Drain.
2. Heat oil in a pot, saut√© onion, garlic, ginger, carrot, potato, and bell pepper until soft.
3. Add spices, cook 1 min. Add tomato and lentils, simmer 10 min. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Grate cucumber, mix with yogurt and chopped coriander for raita.
5. Serve curry hot with raita on the side.

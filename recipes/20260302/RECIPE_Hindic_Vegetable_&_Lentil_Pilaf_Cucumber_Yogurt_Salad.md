# Hindic Vegetable & Lentil Pilaf & Cucumber Yogurt Salad

## Ingredients
- 100g basmati rice
- 80g lentils
- 1 carrot
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 small onion
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1/2 cucumber
- 120g plain yogurt
- 1 tbsp fresh mint
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook lentils and rice together with chopped carrot, bell pepper, onion, and garam masala.
2. Slice cucumber and mix with yogurt, mint, salt, and pepper.
3. Serve pilaf with cucumber yogurt salad on the side.

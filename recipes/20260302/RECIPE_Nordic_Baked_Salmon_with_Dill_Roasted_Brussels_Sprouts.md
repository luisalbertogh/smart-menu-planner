# Nordic Baked Salmon with Dill & Roasted Brussels Sprouts

## Ingredients
- 1 salmon fillet (150g woman, 200g man)
- 1 tbsp fresh dill
- 200g Brussels sprouts
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- Lemon wedges

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
2. Toss Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt, and pepper; roast for 20 minutes.
3. Place salmon on baking tray, sprinkle with dill, salt, and pepper.
4. Bake salmon for 12-15 minutes.
5. Serve with roasted Brussels sprouts and lemon wedges.

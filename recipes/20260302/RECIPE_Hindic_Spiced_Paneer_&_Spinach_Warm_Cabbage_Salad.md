# Hindic Spiced Paneer & Spinach & Warm Cabbage Salad

## Ingredients
- 120g paneer (woman), 160g paneer (man)
- 150g spinach
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 200g cabbage
- 1 carrot
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Slice paneer, sauté with spinach and garam masala in olive oil.
2. Shred cabbage and carrot, sauté until warm, add vinegar, salt, and pepper.
3. Serve spiced paneer & spinach with warm cabbage salad.

# Asiatic Stir-Fried Shrimp & Vegetables & Steamed Edamame Beans

## Ingredients
- 160g shrimp (woman), 200g shrimp (man)
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 carrot
- 1 small broccoli
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 120g edamame beans
- 1 clove garlic
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Steam edamame beans for 5 minutes.
2. Stir-fry chopped vegetables and garlic in sesame oil.
3. Add shrimp, cook until pink, add soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve shrimp stir-fry with steamed edamame beans.

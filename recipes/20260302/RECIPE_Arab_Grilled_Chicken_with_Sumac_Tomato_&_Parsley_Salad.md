# Arab Grilled Chicken with Sumac & Tomato & Parsley Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 chicken breast (120g woman, 160g man)
- 1 tsp sumac
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tomatoes
- 1/2 bunch parsley
- 1/2 small red onion
- Lemon juice
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Marinate chicken in olive oil, sumac, salt, and pepper for 30 minutes.
2. Grill chicken until cooked through.
3. Chop tomatoes, parsley, and onion; toss with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve grilled chicken with tomato & parsley salad.

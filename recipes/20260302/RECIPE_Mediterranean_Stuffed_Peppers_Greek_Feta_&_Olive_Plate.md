# Mediterranean Stuffed Peppers & Greek Feta & Olive Plate

## Ingredients
- 2 large bell peppers
- 120g cooked rice
- 80g chickpeas (cooked)
- 1 small onion
- 1 tomato
- 40g feta cheese (woman)
- 60g feta cheese (man)
- 40g black olives
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
2. Halve and core the peppers.
3. Sauté chopped onion and tomato in olive oil.
4. Mix with rice, chickpeas, oregano, salt, and pepper.
5. Stuff peppers and bake for 25 minutes.
6. Serve with feta and olives on the side.

# Mediterranean Roasted Eggplant & Chickpeas & Minted Green Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 large eggplant
- 120g chickpeas (cooked)
- 1 small onion
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 80g mixed greens
- 1 tbsp fresh mint
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Slice eggplant, brush with olive oil, roast at 200°C for 25 minutes.
2. Sauté onion and garlic, add chickpeas and cumin, cook for 5 minutes.
3. Toss greens with mint, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve roasted eggplant with chickpeas and minted salad.

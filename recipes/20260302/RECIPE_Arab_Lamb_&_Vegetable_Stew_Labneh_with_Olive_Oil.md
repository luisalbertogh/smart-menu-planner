# Arab Lamb & Vegetable Stew & Labneh with Olive Oil

## Ingredients
- 150g lamb (woman), 200g lamb (man)
- 1 carrot
- 1 potato
- 1 zucchini
- 1 small onion
- 1 tomato
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 120g labneh
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for labneh)
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Cut lamb and vegetables into chunks.
2. Sauté onion in olive oil, add lamb and brown.
3. Add vegetables, tomato, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
4. Add water, simmer for 40 minutes.
5. Serve stew with labneh drizzled with olive oil.

# Nordic Fish & Potato Gratin & Pickled Beetroot Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g white fish fillet (woman), 250g (man)
- 200g potatoes
- 1 small leek
- 100ml milk
- 40g cheese
- 1 tbsp butter
- 120g pickled beetroot
- 1 tbsp fresh dill
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Slice potatoes and leek, layer with fish in baking dish.
2. Pour milk over, top with cheese and butter.
3. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
4. Serve with pickled beetroot salad and dill.

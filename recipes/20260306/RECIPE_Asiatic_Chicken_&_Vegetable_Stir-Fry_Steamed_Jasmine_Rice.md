# Asiatic Chicken & Vegetable Stir-Fry & Steamed Jasmine Rice

## Ingredients
- 200g chicken breast
- 1 red bell pepper
- 1 carrot
- 1 small broccoli head
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 garlic clove
- 100g jasmine rice
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook jasmine rice according to package instructions.
2. Slice chicken and vegetables. Stir-fry chicken in oil, add vegetables, garlic, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Cook until just tender. Serve with rice.

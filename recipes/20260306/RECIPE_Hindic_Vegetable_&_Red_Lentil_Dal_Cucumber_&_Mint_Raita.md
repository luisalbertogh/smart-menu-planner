# Hindic Vegetable & Red Lentil Dal & Cucumber & Mint Raita

## Ingredients
- 100g red lentils
- 1 carrot
- 1 small potato
- 1 tomato
- 1/2 onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tsp ground turmeric
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- 200ml water
- 100g Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cucumber
- 1 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion, garlic, carrot, and potato in oil. Add lentils, tomato, spices, and water. Simmer until soft.
2. Grate cucumber, mix with yogurt, mint, salt, and pepper for raita. Serve dal with raita.

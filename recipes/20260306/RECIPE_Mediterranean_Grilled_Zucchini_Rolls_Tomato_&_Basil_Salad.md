# Mediterranean Grilled Zucchini Rolls & Tomato & Basil Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 medium zucchinis
- 50g ricotta cheese
- 1 tbsp pine nuts
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tomatoes
- 1 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
- 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Slice zucchinis lengthwise, grill until soft. Spread ricotta and pine nuts, roll up.
2. Slice tomatoes, toss with basil, olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Serve with zucchini rolls.

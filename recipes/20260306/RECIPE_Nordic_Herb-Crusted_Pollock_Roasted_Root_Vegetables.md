# Nordic Herb-Crusted Pollock & Roasted Root Vegetables

## Ingredients
- 2 pollock fillets (150g each)
- 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 tbsp breadcrumbs
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1 small beetroot
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Toss chopped root vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 30 min.
2. Mix herbs and breadcrumbs, press onto pollock fillets. Bake for 12-15 min. Serve with roasted vegetables.

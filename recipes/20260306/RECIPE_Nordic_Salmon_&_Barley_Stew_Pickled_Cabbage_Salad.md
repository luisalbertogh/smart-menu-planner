# Nordic Salmon & Barley Stew & Pickled Cabbage Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g salmon fillet
- 80g pearl barley
- 1 carrot
- 1 leek
- 1 celery stalk
- 500ml vegetable stock
- 1/4 small cabbage
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook barley in stock with carrot, leek, and celery. Add salmon, simmer until cooked. Stir in dill.
2. Shred cabbage, toss with vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper. Serve stew with pickled cabbage salad.

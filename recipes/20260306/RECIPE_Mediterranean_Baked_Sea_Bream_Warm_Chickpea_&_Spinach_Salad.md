# Mediterranean Baked Sea Bream & Warm Chickpea & Spinach Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 whole sea bream (400g, cleaned)
- 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 sprig fresh thyme
- 200g cooked chickpeas
- 100g fresh spinach
- 1/2 red onion
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Stuff sea bream with lemon slices, garlic, and thyme. Drizzle with olive oil, season, and bake for 20-25 min.
2. For salad: sauté red onion in olive oil, add chickpeas, then spinach. Cook until wilted. Add vinegar, salt, and pepper. Serve warm with fish.

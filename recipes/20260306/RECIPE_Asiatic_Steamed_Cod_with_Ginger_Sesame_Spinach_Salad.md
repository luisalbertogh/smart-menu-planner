# Asiatic Steamed Cod with Ginger & Sesame Spinach Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 cod fillets (120g each)
- 1 thumb ginger
- 1 spring onion
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 200g fresh spinach
- 1 tsp sesame seeds
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Place cod on a plate with ginger and spring onion, steam for 8-10 min. Drizzle with soy sauce.
2. Sauté spinach in oil, add sesame oil and seeds, season. Serve with steamed cod.

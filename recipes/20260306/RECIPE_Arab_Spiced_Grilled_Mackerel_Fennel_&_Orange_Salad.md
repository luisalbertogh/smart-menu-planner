# Arab Spiced Grilled Mackerel & Fennel & Orange Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 mackerel fillets (150g each)
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 fennel bulb
- 1 orange
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Rub mackerel with spices and olive oil. Grill for 3-4 min per side.
2. Slice fennel and orange, toss with lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Serve with grilled fish.

# Arab Chicken & Chickpea Tagine & Carrot & Parsley Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g chicken thigh
- 100g cooked chickpeas
- 1 carrot
- 1/2 onion
- 1 tomato
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 200ml chicken stock
- 2 carrots (for salad)
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion, chicken, and spices in oil. Add tomato, chickpeas, stock, simmer until chicken is tender.
2. Grate carrots, toss with parsley, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Serve tagine with salad.

# Hindic Lentil & Vegetable Curry with Tomato & Onion Raita

## Ingredients
- 150g red lentils
- 1 medium carrot
- 1 small potato
- 1 small zucchini
- 1 small onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 400ml vegetable stock
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tomato
- 1/2 small red onion
- 100g plain yogurt
- 1 tbsp fresh coriander
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Dice vegetables. Heat oil, sauté onion and garlic.
2. Add spices, cook 1 min. Add lentils, vegetables, and stock. Simmer 20 min.
3. Mix diced tomato, red onion, yogurt, coriander, salt, and pepper for raita.
4. Serve curry with raita.

# Nordic Poached Cod with Mustard Sauce and Warm Leek & Potato Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 cod fillets (150g each)
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 lemon
- 1 tsp black peppercorns
- 2 leeks
- 2 medium potatoes
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 2 tbsp crème fraîche
- 1 tbsp chopped dill
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Boil potatoes until tender, slice. Slice leeks and sauté in butter until soft.
2. Combine leeks and potatoes, season with salt and pepper, keep warm.
3. Poach cod in water with bay leaf, lemon slices, and peppercorns for 8 min.
4. Mix mustard, crème fraîche, and dill for sauce.
5. Serve cod with sauce and warm leek & potato salad.

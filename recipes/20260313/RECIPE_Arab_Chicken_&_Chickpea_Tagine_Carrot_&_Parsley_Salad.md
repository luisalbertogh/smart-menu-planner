# Arab Chicken & Chickpea Tagine with Carrot & Parsley Salad

## Ingredients
- 250g chicken thigh, diced
- 200g canned chickpeas
- 1 small onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 courgette
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 200ml chicken stock
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 carrots (for salad)
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil. Add chicken, brown all sides.
2. Add spices, chickpeas, carrot, courgette, and stock. Simmer 25 min.
3. Grate carrots, toss with lemon juice, parsley, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve tagine with carrot & parsley salad.

# Nordic Barley & Root Vegetable Risotto with Pickled Beetroot Salad

## Ingredients
- 150g pearl barley
- 1 carrot
- 1 parsnip
- 1 small turnip
- 1 small onion
- 500ml vegetable stock
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp grated parmesan
- 2 small cooked beetroots
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 1 tbsp fresh dill
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Dice vegetables. Sauté onion in butter, add barley and vegetables.
2. Add stock gradually, cook until barley is tender.
3. Stir in parmesan, season with salt and pepper.
4. Slice beetroots, toss with vinegar and dill.
5. Serve risotto with pickled beetroot salad.

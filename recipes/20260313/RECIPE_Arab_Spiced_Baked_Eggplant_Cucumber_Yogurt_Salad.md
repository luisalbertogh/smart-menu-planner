# Arab Spiced Baked Eggplant with Cucumber Yogurt Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 medium eggplants
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 garlic clove
- 150g Greek yogurt
- 1 small cucumber
- 1 tbsp fresh mint
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Slice eggplants, brush with olive oil, sprinkle with spices and salt.
2. Bake at 200°C for 25 min until golden.
3. Grate cucumber, squeeze excess water, mix with yogurt, minced garlic, mint, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve baked eggplant with cucumber yogurt salad.

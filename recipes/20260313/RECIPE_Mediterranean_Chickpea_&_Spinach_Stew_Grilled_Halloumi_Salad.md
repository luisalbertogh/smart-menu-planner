# Mediterranean Chickpea & Spinach Stew with Grilled Halloumi Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g dried chickpeas (or 400g canned)
- 200g fresh spinach
- 1 medium onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 carrot
- 1 celery stalk
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp cumin
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- 150g halloumi cheese
- 1 small cucumber
- 1 small red onion
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley

## Instructions
1. If using dried chickpeas, soak overnight and cook until tender. If using canned, drain and rinse.
2. Heat olive oil in a pot, sauté onion, garlic, carrot, and celery until soft.
3. Add paprika and cumin, cook 1 min. Add tomatoes and chickpeas, simmer 20 min.
4. Add spinach, cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Slice halloumi, grill until golden.
6. Slice cucumber and red onion, toss with lemon juice and parsley.
7. Serve stew with grilled halloumi salad on the side.

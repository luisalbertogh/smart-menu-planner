# Asiatic Steamed Sea Bass with Sesame Spinach Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 sea bass fillets (120g each)
- 1 thumb ginger
- 2 spring onions
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 200g fresh spinach
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Place sea bass on plate with ginger and spring onions, steam 8 min.
2. Mix soy sauce and sesame oil, drizzle over fish.
3. Wilt spinach, toss with sesame seeds, rice vinegar, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve fish with spinach salad.

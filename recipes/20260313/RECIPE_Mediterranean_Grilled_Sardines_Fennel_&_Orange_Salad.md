# Mediterranean Grilled Sardines with Fennel & Orange Salad

## Ingredients
- 4 fresh sardines, cleaned
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon
- 1 fennel bulb
- 1 orange
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Brush sardines with olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
2. Grill sardines 3-4 min per side.
3. Slice fennel thinly, segment orange, toss with parsley and lemon juice.
4. Serve grilled sardines with fennel & orange salad.

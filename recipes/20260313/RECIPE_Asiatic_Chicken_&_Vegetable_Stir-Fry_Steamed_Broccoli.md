# Asiatic Chicken & Vegetable Stir-Fry with Steamed Broccoli

## Ingredients
- 250g chicken breast
- 1 red bell pepper
- 1 carrot
- 1 small zucchini
- 1 small onion
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tsp ginger, grated
- 200g broccoli
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Slice chicken and vegetables. Heat sesame oil in wok, add garlic and ginger.
2. Add chicken, stir-fry until cooked. Add vegetables, stir-fry 5 min.
3. Add soy sauce, cook 2 min. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
4. Steam broccoli until just tender. Serve stir-fry with broccoli.

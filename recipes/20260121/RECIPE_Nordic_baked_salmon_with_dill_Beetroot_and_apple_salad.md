# Nordic Baked Salmon with Dill & Beetroot and Apple Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 salmon fillets (150g each)
- 2 tbsp fresh dill
- 1 lemon
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 2 small cooked beetroots
- 1 apple
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Place salmon on baking tray, top with dill, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Bake 15 min.
2. Dice beetroot and apple, toss with vinegar, olive oil, and parsley.
3. Serve salmon with salad.

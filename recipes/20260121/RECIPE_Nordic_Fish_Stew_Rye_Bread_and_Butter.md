# Nordic Fish Stew & Rye Bread and Butter

## Ingredients
- 250g white fish fillets (cod or haddock)
- 1 small leek
- 1 carrot
- 1 small potato
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 cup fish stock
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 slices rye bread
- 1 tbsp fresh dill
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Slice leek, carrot, and potato. Saut√© in butter for 3 minutes.
2. Add fish stock and milk, simmer 10 minutes.
3. Add fish, cook until just done (5 minutes). Season with salt, pepper, and dill.
4. Serve with rye bread and butter on the side.

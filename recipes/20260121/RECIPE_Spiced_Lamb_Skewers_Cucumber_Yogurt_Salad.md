# Spiced Lamb Skewers & Cucumber Yogurt Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g lamb leg, cubed
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 small cucumber
- 100g Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp chopped mint
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Toss lamb with spices, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Thread onto skewers.
2. Grill or broil skewers for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally.
3. Dice cucumber, mix with yogurt and mint.
4. Serve lamb skewers with cucumber yogurt salad.

# Veal and chickpea stew & Roasted root vegetables

## Ingredients
- 200g veal stew meat
- 100g cooked chickpeas
- 1 onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 parsnip
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 400ml beef broth
- 1 potato
- 1 beetroot
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Brown veal in olive oil. Add onion, carrot, parsnip, and cook for 5 minutes.
2. Stir in tomato paste, chickpeas, and beef broth. Simmer for 40 minutes.
3. Chop potato and beetroot, toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 200Â°C for 25 minutes.
4. Serve stew with roasted vegetables.

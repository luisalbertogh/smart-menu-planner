# Salmon Teriyaki with Steamed Green Beans

## Ingredients
- 180g salmon fillet
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 1 tsp honey
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 120g green beans
- 1 tsp sesame seeds
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Mix soy sauce, mirin, honey, and sesame oil. Marinate salmon 10 min.
2. Steam green beans 5 min, season with salt.

4. Serve salmon with green beans, sprinkle with sesame seeds.

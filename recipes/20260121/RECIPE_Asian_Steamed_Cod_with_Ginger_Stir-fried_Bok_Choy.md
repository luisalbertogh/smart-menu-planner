# Asian Steamed Cod with Ginger & Stir-fried Bok Choy

## Ingredients
- 2 cod fillets (120g each)
- 1 thumb ginger
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 2 heads bok choy
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Place cod fillets on a plate, top with sliced ginger and garlic, drizzle with soy sauce and rice vinegar. Steam for 8 minutes.
2. Chop bok choy, stir-fry in vegetable oil with a pinch of salt for 3 minutes.
3. Drizzle cod with sesame oil and serve with bok choy.

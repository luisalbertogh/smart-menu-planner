# Grilled lamb kofta & Tomato and cucumber salad

## Ingredients
- 200g ground lamb
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- Salt and pepper
- 1 tomato
- 1/2 cucumber
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Mix lamb with garlic, cumin, coriander, parsley, salt, and pepper. Shape into small logs.
2. Grill koftas for 8 minutes, turning halfway.
3. Chop tomato and cucumber, toss with olive oil and lemon juice.
4. Serve koftas with salad.

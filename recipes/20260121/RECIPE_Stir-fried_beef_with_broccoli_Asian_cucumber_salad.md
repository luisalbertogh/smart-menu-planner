# Stir-fried Beef with Broccoli & Asian Cucumber Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g beef sirloin
- 200g broccoli
- 1 red bell pepper
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt and pepper
- 1 cucumber
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame seeds
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp chopped cilantro

## Instructions
1. Slice beef thinly, marinate with soy, oyster sauce, cornstarch.
2. Stir-fry beef in oil, remove. Stir-fry broccoli, pepper, garlic. Return beef, toss with sesame oil.
3. For salad: Slice cucumber, toss with vinegar, sugar, sesame seeds, cilantro.
4. Serve hot beef and broccoli with salad.

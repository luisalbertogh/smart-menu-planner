# Pasta with Sardines and Lemon with Roasted Peppers

## Ingredients
- 120g pasta (dry)
- 1 can sardines in olive oil (90g drained)
- 1 lemon
- 1 small red bell pepper
- 1 small yellow bell pepper
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Roast bell peppers at 220°C (430°F) for 20 min, peel and slice.
2. Cook pasta in salted water, drain.
3. Toss pasta with sardines, lemon zest, juice, parsley, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve with roasted peppers on the side.

# Grilled Sea Bass with Herbs & Roasted Brussels Sprouts

## Ingredients
- 2 sea bass fillets (150g each)
- 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1 tbsp chopped dill
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 300g Brussels sprouts
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for sprouts)
- 1 garlic clove

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Halve Brussels sprouts, toss with olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20 min.
2. Brush sea bass with olive oil, sprinkle with herbs, salt, and pepper. Grill for 3-4 min per side.
3. Serve fish with lemon wedges and roasted sprouts.

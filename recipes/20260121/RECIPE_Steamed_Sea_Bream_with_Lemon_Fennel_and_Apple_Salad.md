# Steamed Sea Bream with Lemon & Fennel and Apple Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 sea bream fillets (150g each)
- 1 lemon
- 1 small fennel bulb
- 1 green apple
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp fresh dill
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Season sea bream fillets with salt, pepper, and half the lemon juice.
2. Steam fillets for 8-10 minutes until cooked through.
3. Thinly slice fennel and apple, toss with olive oil, remaining lemon juice, and dill.
4. Serve sea bream with fennel and apple salad on the side.

# Pasta with spinach and ricotta & Marinated artichokes

## Ingredients
- 120g pasta (fusilli or penne)
- 100g spinach
- 100g ricotta cheese
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 4 marinated artichoke hearts

## Instructions
1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
2. Saut√© garlic in olive oil, add spinach and cook until wilted.
3. Mix ricotta with spinach, season with salt and pepper.
4. Toss pasta with spinach-ricotta mixture.
5. Serve with marinated artichoke hearts on the side.

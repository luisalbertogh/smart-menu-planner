# Chicken and lentil curry (Arab style) & Cabbage and carrot slaw

## Ingredients
- 200g chicken breast
- 80g brown lentils
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 400ml chicken broth
- 1/4 cabbage
- 1 carrot
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Dice chicken breast. Saut√© onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
2. Add chicken, curry powder, cumin, and coriander. Cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add lentils and chicken broth. Simmer for 25 minutes until lentils are tender.
4. Shred cabbage and carrot, toss with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
5. Serve curry with slaw on the side.

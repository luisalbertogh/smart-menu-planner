# Chicken and Chickpea Tagine & Carrot and Orange Salad

## Ingredients
- 250g chicken thigh, diced
- 100g cooked chickpeas
- 1 small onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 small zucchini
- 1 tomato
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- 1 orange
- 2 carrots (for salad)
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Saut√© onion in olive oil, add chicken and brown.
2. Add spices, tomato, carrot, zucchini, chickpeas, and broth. Simmer 25 minutes.
3. Grate carrots for salad, segment orange, toss with lemon juice and parsley.
4. Serve tagine hot with carrot and orange salad on the side.

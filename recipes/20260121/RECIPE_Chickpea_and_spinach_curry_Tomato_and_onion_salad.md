# Chickpea and Spinach Curry & Tomato and Onion Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 can chickpeas (400g)
- 100g spinach
- 1 small onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp ginger
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1 can chopped tomatoes (400g)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 2 tomatoes
- 1/2 small red onion
- 1 tbsp fresh coriander
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger in oil. Add curry powder, chickpeas, tomatoes, and simmer 15 min.
2. Add spinach, cook 3 min. Season.
3. For salad: slice tomatoes and onion, toss with coriander and lemon juice.
4. Serve curry with salad.

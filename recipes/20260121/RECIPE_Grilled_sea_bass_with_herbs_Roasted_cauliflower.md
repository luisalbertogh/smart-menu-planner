# Grilled Sea Bass with Herbs & Roasted Cauliflower

## Ingredients
- 2 sea bass fillets (150g each)
- 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley
- 1 tbsp fresh dill
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 1 small cauliflower
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Cut cauliflower into florets, toss with olive oil, paprika, salt, and pepper. Roast 25 min.
2. Mix chopped herbs, lemon zest, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Rub over sea bass.
3. Grill sea bass 3-4 min per side until cooked.
4. Serve with roasted cauliflower.

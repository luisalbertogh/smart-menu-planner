# Grilled Zucchini with Herbed Ricotta and Arugula-Radish Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 medium zucchini
- 80g ricotta cheese
- 1 tbsp chopped herbs (basil, parsley)
- 1 cup arugula
- 3 radishes
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Slice zucchini, brush with olive oil, grill 2â€“3 min per side.
2. Mix ricotta with herbs, salt, and pepper.
3. Slice radishes, toss with arugula, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve grilled zucchini with herbed ricotta and salad.

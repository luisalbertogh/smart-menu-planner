# Vegetable Moussaka & Tzatziki with Olives

## Ingredients
- 1 eggplant
- 1 zucchini
- 2 potatoes
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 100ml milk
- 1 egg
- 50g grated cheese
- Salt and pepper
- 100g Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cucumber
- 1 garlic clove (for tzatziki)
- 8 Kalamata olives
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for tzatziki)
- 1 tbsp chopped dill

## Instructions
1. Slice eggplant, zucchini, potatoes. Roast with olive oil at 200°C for 20 min.
2. Sauté onion and garlic, add tomatoes, oregano, cinnamon. Simmer 10 min.
3. Layer vegetables and sauce in dish. Mix milk, egg, cheese, pour on top. Bake 25 min.
4. For tzatziki: Grate cucumber, mix with yogurt, garlic, dill, olive oil. Serve with olives.

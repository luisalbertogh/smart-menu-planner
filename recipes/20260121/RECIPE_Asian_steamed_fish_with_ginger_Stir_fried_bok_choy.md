# Asian steamed fish with ginger & Stir-fried bok choy

## Ingredients
- 2 white fish fillets (150g each)
- 1 thumb ginger
- 2 spring onions
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 200g bok choy
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Place fish fillets on a plate, top with sliced ginger and spring onions.
2. Steam for 10 minutes until cooked. Drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil.
3. Slice bok choy, stir-fry with garlic in vegetable oil for 3 minutes.
4. Serve fish with bok choy.

# Grilled Eggplant Rolls with Ricotta & Arugula and Walnut Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 large eggplant
- 100g ricotta cheese
- 1 tbsp chopped basil
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 40g arugula
- 20g walnuts
- 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Slice eggplant lengthwise, brush with olive oil, grill until tender.
2. Mix ricotta with basil, salt, and pepper. Spread on eggplant slices and roll up.
3. Toss arugula with walnuts, balsamic vinegar, and a pinch of salt.
4. Serve eggplant rolls with arugula and walnut salad.

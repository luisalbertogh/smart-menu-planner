# Chicken Tagine with Chickpeas and Carrot-Orange Salad

## Ingredients
- 180g chicken thigh, boneless
- 100g cooked chickpeas
- 1 small onion
- 1 small carrot
- 1 orange
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp ras el hanout
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Brown chicken in olive oil. Add onion, ras el hanout, cumin, coriander, salt, and pepper. Cook 5 min.
2. Add chickpeas, 100ml water. Simmer 20 min.
3. Grate carrot, segment orange, toss with parsley and a pinch of salt.
4. Serve tagine with carrot-orange salad.

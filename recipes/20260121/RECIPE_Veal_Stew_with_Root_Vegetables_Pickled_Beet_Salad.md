# Veal Stew with Root Vegetables and Pickled Beet Salad

## Ingredients
- 180g veal stew meat
- 1 small carrot
- 1 small parsnip
- 1 small potato
- 1 small beet
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tsp thyme
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Brown veal in olive oil. Add diced carrot, parsnip, potato, bay leaf, thyme, salt, and pepper. Cover with water, simmer 45 min.
2. Boil beet until tender, peel and slice. Toss with vinegar, salt, and pepper.
3. Serve stew with pickled beet salad.

# Stir-fried Veal with Ginger and Broccoli & Asian Cucumber Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g veal strips
- 1 small broccoli
- 1 small carrot
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 small cucumber
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame seeds
- 1 tsp honey

## Instructions
1. Slice veal, broccoli, carrot, ginger, and garlic.
2. Stir-fry veal in olive oil, add ginger and garlic, then vegetables. Add soy sauce and sesame oil, cook 5 min.
3. For salad: slice cucumber, toss with vinegar, sesame seeds, and honey.
4. Serve stir-fry with salad.

# Grilled Halloumi with Roasted Vegetables & Tomato and Mint Salad

## Ingredients
- 200g halloumi cheese
- 1 red bell pepper
- 1 zucchini
- 1 eggplant
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 2 tomatoes
- 1/2 red onion
- 1 tbsp chopped mint
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for salad)
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Slice and roast vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper at 200Â°C for 20 min.
2. Grill halloumi slices 2 min per side.
3. For salad: Slice tomatoes, onion, toss with mint, olive oil, lemon juice.
4. Serve grilled halloumi with roasted vegetables and salad.

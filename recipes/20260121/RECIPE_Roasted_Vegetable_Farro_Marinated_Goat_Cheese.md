# Roasted Vegetable Farro with Marinated Goat Cheese

## Ingredients
- 80g farro (dry)
- 1 small zucchini
- 1 small red bell pepper
- 1 small eggplant
- 1 small red onion
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 60g goat cheese
- 1 tbsp fresh herbs (thyme, parsley)
- 1 tsp red wine vinegar
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F). Dice zucchini, bell pepper, eggplant, and onion. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 25 min.
2. Cook farro in salted water for 20 min, drain.
3. Mix roasted vegetables with farro and herbs.
4. Marinate goat cheese with 1 tbsp olive oil, vinegar, and herbs for 10 min.
5. Serve farro warm with marinated goat cheese on the side.

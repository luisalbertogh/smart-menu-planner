# Roasted Vegetable Farro & Marinated Goat Cheese

## Ingredients
- 120g farro
- 1 small zucchini
- 1 red bell pepper
- 1 small eggplant
- 1 small red onion
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 80g goat cheese
- 1 tbsp fresh thyme
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F). Dice zucchini, bell pepper, eggplant, and onion.
2. Toss vegetables with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper, and thyme. Roast for 25 minutes.
3. Cook farro in salted water for 20 minutes, drain.
4. Mix roasted vegetables with farro and balsamic vinegar.
5. Slice goat cheese, drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil, and marinate while vegetables roast.
6. Serve farro with marinated goat cheese on the side.

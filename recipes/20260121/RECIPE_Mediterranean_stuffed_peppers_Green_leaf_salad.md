# Mediterranean stuffed peppers & Green leaf salad

## Ingredients
- 2 bell peppers
- 80g cooked rice
- 80g cooked lentils
- 1 tomato
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 100g mixed green leaves
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Cut tops off peppers, remove seeds. Mix rice, lentils, chopped tomato, parsley, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
2. Stuff peppers with mixture, bake at 180Â°C for 25 minutes.
3. Toss green leaves with olive oil and lemon juice.
4. Serve stuffed peppers with salad.

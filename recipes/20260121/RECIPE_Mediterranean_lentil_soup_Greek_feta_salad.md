# Mediterranean Lentil Soup & Greek Feta Salad

## Ingredients
- 120g brown lentils
- 1 medium onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 celery stalk
- 2 garlic cloves
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 1L vegetable broth
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper
- 100g feta cheese
- 2 medium tomatoes
- 1/2 cucumber
- 1/2 red onion
- 8 Kalamata olives
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for salad)
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano

## Instructions
1. Rinse lentils. Chop onion, carrot, celery, and garlic.
2. Heat olive oil in a pot, saut√© onion, carrot, celery, and garlic until soft.
3. Add cumin and paprika, stir for 1 min. Add lentils, canned tomatoes, and broth.
4. Simmer for 30 min until lentils are tender. Season with salt and pepper.
5. For salad: Chop tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion. Mix with olives, feta, oregano, vinegar, and olive oil.
6. Serve soup hot with salad on the side.

# Vegetable Tagine & Labneh with Za'atar

## Ingredients
- 1 small eggplant
- 1 zucchini
- 1 carrot
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 small onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 can chickpeas (400g)
- 1 can chopped tomatoes (400g)
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 120g labneh
- 1 tsp za'atar
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Chop all vegetables. Saut√© onion and garlic in oil, add spices, then vegetables. Cook 5 min.
2. Add chickpeas and tomatoes, simmer 25 min. Season.
3. For labneh: drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle za'atar.
4. Serve tagine hot with labneh.

# Nordic baked haddock with lemon & Pickled beetroot salad

## Ingredients
- 2 haddock fillets (150g each)
- 1 lemon
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 2 medium beetroots
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp chopped dill

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Place haddock fillets on a baking tray, drizzle with olive oil, squeeze lemon juice, and season.
2. Bake for 15 minutes until fish is cooked through.
3. Boil beetroots until tender, peel and slice.
4. Mix beetroot slices with vinegar, sugar, and dill. Serve fish with beetroot salad.

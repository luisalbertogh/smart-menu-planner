# Greek Fish Stew & Horiatiki Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 white fish fillets (cod or haddock, 150g each)
- 1 small onion
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 tomato
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 400ml fish stock
- Salt and pepper
- 100g feta cheese
- 1 small cucumber
- 2 tomatoes
- 1/2 small red onion
- 8 Kalamata olives
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar

## Instructions
1. Chop onion, pepper, tomato, and garlic. Saut√© in oil, add oregano, then fish and stock. Simmer 10 min.
2. For salad: chop cucumber, tomatoes, onion, olives, and feta. Toss with oil and vinegar.
3. Serve stew with salad.

# Mediterranean Lentil Soup & Feta and Olive Salad

## Ingredients
- 120g brown lentils
- 1 medium onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 celery stalk
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 can chopped tomatoes (400g)
- 1L vegetable broth
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- Salt and pepper
- 100g feta cheese
- 80g Kalamata olives
- 1 small cucumber
- 1 small red onion
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Rinse lentils. Chop onion, carrot, celery, and garlic.
2. Heat olive oil in a pot, saut√© onion, carrot, celery, and garlic until soft.
3. Add cumin and paprika, stir for 1 min. Add lentils, tomatoes, and broth.
4. Simmer 30 min until lentils are tender. Season with salt and pepper.
5. For salad: slice cucumber and onion, mix with olives, crumbled feta, parsley, and lemon juice.
6. Serve soup hot with salad on the side.

# Hindic spiced rice with vegetables & Paneer and bell pepper

## Ingredients
- 120g basmati rice
- 1 carrot
- 1/2 cup peas
- 1/2 onion
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- 100g paneer
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook rice with turmeric and garam masala.
2. Sauté onion, carrot, and peas in vegetable oil until soft. Mix with rice.
3. Cube paneer and bell pepper, sauté in olive oil for 5 minutes.
4. Serve spiced rice with paneer and bell pepper.

# Chicken Biryani & Cucumber Raita

## Ingredients
- 180g basmati rice
- 250g chicken breast
- 1 onion
- 1 tomato
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp ginger
- 2 tbsp biryani spice mix
- 2 tbsp yogurt
- 2 tbsp oil
- 350ml chicken broth
- Salt and pepper
- 1/2 cucumber
- 100g yogurt (for raita)
- 1 tbsp chopped mint
- 1/2 tsp cumin

## Instructions
1. Rinse rice. Slice onion, tomato, garlic, and ginger.
2. Saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger in oil. Add chicken, brown, then add tomato and biryani spices.
3. Stir in rice, yogurt, and broth. Simmer covered 20 min.
4. For raita: Grate cucumber, mix with yogurt, mint, and cumin.
5. Serve biryani hot with raita.

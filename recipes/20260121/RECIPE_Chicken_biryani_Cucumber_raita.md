# Chicken Biryani & Cucumber Raita

## Ingredients
- 180g basmati rice
- 250g chicken breast
- 1 medium onion
- 1 tomato
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp ginger
- 2 tbsp biryani spice mix
- 2 tbsp yogurt
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 350ml chicken broth
- Salt and pepper
- 1 small cucumber
- 120g Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp fresh mint
- 1/2 tsp cumin

## Instructions
1. Rinse rice. Slice onion, tomato, garlic, and ginger.
2. Saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger in oil. Add chicken, brown, then add tomato and biryani spice.
3. Stir in rice, yogurt, and broth. Simmer covered 20 min until rice is cooked.
4. For raita: grate cucumber, mix with yogurt, mint, and cumin.
5. Serve biryani hot with raita.

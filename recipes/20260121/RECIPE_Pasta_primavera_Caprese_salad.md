# Pasta Primavera & Caprese Salad

## Ingredients
- 160g penne pasta
- 1 small zucchini
- 1 small carrot
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 small onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 can cherry tomatoes (200g)
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper
- 120g mozzarella
- 2 tomatoes
- 10 basil leaves
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Cook pasta. Saut√© onion, garlic, and vegetables in oil. Add tomatoes and oregano, cook 5 min.
2. Toss pasta with vegetables, season.
3. For salad: slice mozzarella and tomatoes, layer with basil, drizzle with oil.
4. Serve together.

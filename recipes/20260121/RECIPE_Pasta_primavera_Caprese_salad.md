# Pasta Primavera & Caprese Salad

## Ingredients
- 180g pasta (penne or fusilli)
- 1 zucchini
- 1 carrot
- 1 bell pepper
- 1/2 cup peas
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 garlic clove
- 30g parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper
- 2 tomatoes
- 100g mozzarella
- 8 basil leaves
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for salad)
- Salt and pepper (for salad)

## Instructions
1. Cook pasta. Saut√© chopped vegetables and garlic in olive oil until tender.
2. Toss pasta with vegetables, season, top with parmesan.
3. For salad: Slice tomatoes and mozzarella, layer with basil, drizzle with olive oil, season.
4. Serve pasta with caprese salad.

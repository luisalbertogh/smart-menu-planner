# Grilled Eggplant with Tahini and Tomato-Cucumber Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 medium eggplant
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp tahini
- 1 small tomato
- 1/2 cucumber
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Slice eggplant, brush with olive oil, grill 3â€“4 min per side.
2. Mix tahini with lemon juice, salt, and a little water to thin.
3. Dice tomato and cucumber, toss with parsley, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve grilled eggplant with tahini sauce and salad.

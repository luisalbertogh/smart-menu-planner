# Veal and Mushroom Risotto & Endive and Pear Salad

## Ingredients
- 120g arborio rice
- 150g veal, diced
- 80g mushrooms
- 1 small onion
- 1 clove garlic
- 500ml chicken broth
- 30g parmesan cheese
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 small endive
- 1 pear
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Saut√© onion and garlic in olive oil, add veal and brown.
2. Add rice, stir, then add broth gradually, stirring until absorbed.
3. Add mushrooms halfway, cook until rice is creamy. Stir in parmesan.
4. Slice endive and pear, toss with lemon juice. Serve risotto with salad.

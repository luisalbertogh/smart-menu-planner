# Steamed Sea Bream with Lemon and Shaved Fennel Salad

## Ingredients
- 2 sea bream fillets (120g each)
- 1 lemon
- 1 small fennel bulb
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp fresh dill
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Season sea bream with salt, pepper, and lemon zest. Steam for 8â€“10 min.
2. Thinly slice fennel. Toss with olive oil, lemon juice, dill, salt, and pepper.
3. Serve fish with fennel salad on the side.

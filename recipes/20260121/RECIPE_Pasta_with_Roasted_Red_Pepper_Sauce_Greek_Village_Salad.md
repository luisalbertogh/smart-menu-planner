# Pasta with Roasted Red Pepper Sauce & Greek Village Salad

## Ingredients
- 120g pasta (fusilli or penne)
- 2 red bell peppers
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 40g feta cheese
- 1 tomato
- 1/2 cucumber
- 1/4 red onion
- 6 Kalamata olives
- 1 tsp oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Roast bell peppers, peel and blend with garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
2. Cook pasta, toss with pepper sauce.
3. Chop tomato, cucumber, onion, and olives. Toss with feta and oregano.
4. Serve pasta with Greek salad on the side.

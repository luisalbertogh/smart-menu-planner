# Roasted vegetable barley risotto & Greek yogurt with herbs

## Ingredients
- 120g pearl barley
- 1 medium zucchini
- 1 red bell pepper
- 1 carrot
- 1 small onion
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 500ml vegetable broth
- 100g Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
- 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Chop zucchini, bell pepper, carrot, and onion into bite-sized pieces.
2. Toss vegetables with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20 minutes.
3. In a pot, heat 1 tbsp olive oil. Add barley and stir for 2 minutes.
4. Add vegetable broth and simmer for 25 minutes until barley is tender.
5. Stir roasted vegetables into barley and cook for 5 more minutes.
6. Mix Greek yogurt with dill and parsley. Serve risotto with a dollop of herbed yogurt.

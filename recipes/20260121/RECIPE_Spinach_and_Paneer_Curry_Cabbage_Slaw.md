# Spinach and Paneer Curry with Cabbage Slaw

## Ingredients
- 120g paneer cheese
- 200g spinach
- 1 small onion
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 tsp ginger
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 small cabbage
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger in oil. Add spinach, cook until wilted.
2. Add paneer cubes, garam masala, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Simmer 5 min.
3. Shred cabbage, toss with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve curry with cabbage slaw.

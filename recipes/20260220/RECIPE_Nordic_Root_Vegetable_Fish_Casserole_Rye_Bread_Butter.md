# Nordic Root Vegetable & Fish Casserole & Rye Bread & Butter

## Ingredients
- White fish fillets (cod/haddock): 2 (man), 1.5 (woman)
- Potato: 2 medium
- Carrot: 2 medium
- Parsnip: 1
- Leek: 1
- Cream: 80ml
- Butter: 30g
- Rye bread: 2 slices (man), 1 slice (woman)
- Salt & pepper: to taste

## Instructions
1. Layer sliced vegetables and fish in a casserole dish.
2. Pour cream over, dot with butter.
3. Bake at 180°C for 30 min.
4. Serve with rye bread and butter.

# Hindic Chickpea & Potato Curry & Labneh with Olive Oil

## Ingredients
- Chickpeas: 120g (man), 100g (woman)
- Potato: 1 large
- Onion: 1
- Tomato: 1
- Garlic: 2 cloves
- Curry powder: 1 tbsp
- Olive oil: 2 tbsp
- Labneh: 60g (man), 40g (woman)
- Olive oil: 1 tbsp
- Salt & pepper: to taste
- Fresh coriander: handful

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion, garlic, and tomato in olive oil.
2. Add potato, chickpeas, curry powder, cook for 20 min.
3. Season with salt, pepper, and coriander.
4. Serve curry with labneh drizzled with olive oil.

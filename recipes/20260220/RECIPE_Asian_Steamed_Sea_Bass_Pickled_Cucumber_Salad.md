# Asian Steamed Sea Bass & Pickled Cucumber Salad

## Ingredients
- Sea bass fillets: 2 (man), 1.5 (woman)
- Ginger: 1 tbsp
- Soy sauce: 2 tbsp
- Spring onion: 2
- Cucumber: 1
- Rice vinegar: 2 tbsp
- Sugar: 1 tsp
- Salt: to taste

## Instructions
1. Steam sea bass fillets with ginger and spring onion for 8 min.
2. Slice cucumber, mix with vinegar, sugar, and salt for 10 min.
3. Serve steamed sea bass with pickled cucumber salad.

# Italian Minestrone Vegetable Stew & Pecorino Cheese Plate

## Ingredients
- Olive oil: 2 tbsp
- Onion: 1 medium
- Carrot: 2 medium
- Celery: 2 stalks
- Potato: 1 large
- Zucchini: 1 medium
- Green beans: 100g
- Canned tomatoes: 400g
- Cannellini beans: 200g
- Vegetable broth: 600ml
- Garlic: 2 cloves
- Pecorino cheese: 80g (man), 60g (woman)
- Salt & pepper: to taste
- Fresh basil: handful

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a pot, sauté onion, garlic, carrot, and celery for 5 min.
2. Add potato, zucchini, green beans, and cook for 5 min.
3. Add tomatoes, broth, and cannellini beans. Simmer for 25 min.
4. Season with salt, pepper, and basil.
5. Serve hot with sliced Pecorino cheese on the side.

# Arab Grilled Lamb Chops & Tomato & Mint Salad

## Ingredients
- Lamb chops: 2 (man), 1.5 (woman)
- Olive oil: 1 tbsp
- Garlic: 1 clove
- Tomato: 2
- Fresh mint: handful
- Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
- Salt & pepper: to taste

## Instructions
1. Rub lamb chops with olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
2. Grill for 4 min per side.
3. Slice tomatoes, mix with mint and lemon juice.
4. Serve lamb chops with tomato & mint salad.

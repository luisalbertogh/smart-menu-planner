# Arab Lentil & Spinach Pilaf & Feta & Olive Plate

## Ingredients
- Brown lentils: 120g (man), 100g (woman)
- Basmati rice: 80g (man), 60g (woman)
- Spinach: 120g
- Onion: 1
- Olive oil: 2 tbsp
- Garlic: 2 cloves
- Cumin: 1 tsp
- Vegetable broth: 400ml
- Feta cheese: 60g (man), 40g (woman)
- Kalamata olives: 10
- Salt & pepper: to taste

## Instructions
1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
2. Add lentils, cumin, and broth. Simmer for 15 min.
3. Add rice and cook for 10 min.
4. Stir in spinach, cook until wilted.
5. Serve pilaf with feta and olives on the side.

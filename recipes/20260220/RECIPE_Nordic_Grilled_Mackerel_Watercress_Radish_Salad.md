# Nordic Grilled Mackerel & Watercress & Radish Salad

## Ingredients
- Mackerel fillets: 2 (man), 1.5 (woman)
- Olive oil: 1 tbsp
- Lemon: 1
- Watercress: 80g
- Radish: 6
- Salt & pepper: to taste

## Instructions
1. Brush mackerel fillets with olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
2. Grill fillets for 3-4 min per side.
3. Toss watercress and sliced radish with lemon juice.
4. Serve grilled mackerel with salad.

# Mediterranean Roasted Vegetable Pasta & Greek Yogurt & Herb Dip

## Ingredients
- Penne pasta: 80g (man), 60g (woman)
- Eggplant: 1 small
- Bell pepper: 1
- Zucchini: 1
- Cherry tomatoes: 100g
- Olive oil: 2 tbsp
- Garlic: 2 cloves
- Greek yogurt: 60g (man), 40g (woman)
- Fresh dill: 1 tbsp
- Fresh mint: 1 tbsp
- Salt & pepper: to taste

## Instructions
1. Roast chopped eggplant, pepper, zucchini, tomatoes with olive oil and garlic at 200°C for 20 min.
2. Cook pasta, toss with roasted vegetables.
3. Mix yogurt with dill and mint for dip.
4. Serve pasta with yogurt dip on the side.

# Hindic Tandoori Chicken Skewers & Roasted Cauliflower Salad

## Ingredients
- Chicken breast: 180g (man), 140g (woman)
- Greek yogurt: 60g
- Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
- Tandoori spice mix: 2 tsp
- Cauliflower: 200g
- Olive oil: 1 tbsp
- Fresh coriander: handful
- Salt & pepper: to taste

## Instructions
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, tandoori spice, salt for 1 hour.
2. Thread chicken onto skewers and grill for 8 min, turning.
3. Toss cauliflower florets with olive oil, roast at 200°C for 20 min.
4. Sprinkle roasted cauliflower with coriander.
5. Serve chicken skewers with cauliflower salad.
